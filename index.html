A Mechanical Engineer with an MBA from IIM Ahmedabad, Amit Krishnan has spent years asking the question that most of the scientific establishment has stopped asking: not whether our equations are correct, but whether they are complete.

The Physical Universe series is his attempt to lay out, step by step, a physical description of the Universe that can explain observations — from gravity to elasticity to atomic energy thresholds — not as the outputs of mathematical abstractions, but as the outcomes of real, physical processes. Processes that, once understood, could change everything.
